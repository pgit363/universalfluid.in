What is a fluid technology?

Fluid Technology – The technology of using fluid, either gases (pneumatics) or liquid (hydraulics) to apply force or to transport.
(Airbrakes on a truck, tires on a bike, hydraulic jack, plumbing, air ducts and fans) Fluid Tech Notes.
